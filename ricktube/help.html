Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a video?

To upload a video, navigate to the "Upload" button in your account dashboard. Follow the steps to select, title, and publish your video to RickTube.

How do I subscribe to RickTube Premium?

Visit the Premium Page to learn about exclusive features and subscribe.

How do I change my account settings?

Access your account settings by clicking on your profile icon in the upper right corner and selecting "Settings."
